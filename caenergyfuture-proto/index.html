Our vision

Our vision is to be the cleanest natural gas utility in North America, delivering affordable and increasingly renewable energy to our customers. Natural gas has played a role in improving air quality, particulary in electric generation as natural pas replaced coal. AS the state works to achieve its climate change goals, natural gas along with renewable natural gas and hydrogen will serve as energy sources to help California advance it clean energy agenda.

current technologies

The future of clean and renewable energy relies on current and future undiscovered technologies. Each year new advances in energy technology help deliver increasingly more renewable energy. However, we can’t just rely on future technology. We need technology today that is Ready, Reliable and Realistic. Solar and wind in conjunction with renewable natural gas play a critical role today in providing clean energy and decarbonizing energy in California. Learning about current and future technologies that will help reduce emission in state keeps you educating on aff ordable energy choices. By optimizing all energy technology possibilities, we create a more balanced system to meet the state’s energy demand.
